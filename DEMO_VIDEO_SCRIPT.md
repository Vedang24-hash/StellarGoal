## 🎥 Recording Tips

### Before Recording
1. **Practice**: Run through the script 2-3 times
2. **Prepare**: Have all URLs and data ready
3. **Test**: Ensure Freighter works smoothly
4. **Clean**: Close unnecessary apps and notifications
5. **Lighting**: Good lighting for webcam (if showing face)

### During Recording
1. **Pace**: Speak clearly and not too fast
2. **Pause**: Brief pauses between sections
3. **Show**: Use cursor to point at important elements
4. **Highlight**: Circle or highlight key features (editing)
5. **Smooth**: Avoid long waits or loading times

### After Recording
1. **Edit**: Cut any mistakes or long pauses
2. **Add**: On-screen text annotations
3. **Enhance**: Add background music (low volume)
4. **Export**: 1080p MP4 format
5. **Upload**: YouTube unlisted or public
